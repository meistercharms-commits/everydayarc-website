Notice to All Visitors

Welcome to the Department

The Department of Small Matters exists so that no small matter need remain entirely unwitnessed.

This Office receives grievances, commendations, public conduct observations and related matters that require formal acknowledgement. All matters, however modest, receive the same measure of attention and a lasting entry in the register.

Whereas a matter has been brought before this Office, the Department does hereby record and certify the following.

The claimant's grievance was received, considered and entered upon the record. The Department finds the complaint to be real, the slight to be noted, and the experience entirely deserving of acknowledgement.

Let it be known that the matter, however minor, has been seen.

Duly filed this day, in the year two thousand and twenty-six.
